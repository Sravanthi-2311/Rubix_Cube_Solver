Rubik's Cube Solver

How to Use the Camera Solver

  1. Click Start Camera Scan and allow camera access.
  2. Align your Rubik's Cube within the on-screen guides.
  3. Show the face with the WHITE center and press the SPACEBAR.
  4. Follow the on-screen prompts to scan the remaining 5 faces in order.
  5. Once all faces are scanned, the Solve Cube button will activate.
  6. The app will generate the solution and animate it on the virtual cube below.
